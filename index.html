Outer Wilds Explorer

Journey through the mysteries of the Outer Wilds universe

The Solar System

Explore the unique and mysterious planets of the Outer Wilds system, each with its own secrets and dangers.

Timber Hearth

Home to the Hearthian civilization, this lush planet features dense forests, winding rivers, and the iconic Timber Hearth village.

Brittle Hollow

A fragile planet with a hollow interior, slowly crumbling into its black hole core. Home to the ancient Nomai ruins.

Giant's Deep

A water-covered planet with massive cyclones and mysterious islands floating in its atmosphere.

Dark Bramble

A dangerous planet covered in fog and filled with interconnected pockets of space. Home to the terrifying Anglerfish.

The Nomai

The Nomai were an ancient, highly advanced civilization that once inhabited the Outer Wilds system. Their ruins and technology can be found throughout the solar system, telling the story of their search for the Eye of the Universe.

Their writing system, architecture, and technology reveal a culture focused on scientific discovery and exploration.

The Time Loop

Every 22 minutes, the sun goes supernova, resetting the universe to the beginning of the loop. This mysterious phenomenon is tied to the Ash Twin Project and the Nomai's search for the Eye of the Universe.

As an explorer, you must use this time loop to your advantage, learning from each cycle to uncover the secrets of the solar system.

Exploration Tips

Ship Controls

Master the art of zero-gravity navigation. Remember to use your landing camera and match velocity when approaching planets.

Time Management

Plan your exploration carefully within the 22-minute time loop. Some events only occur at specific times.

Signal Tracking

Use your signalscope to track important signals and locate points of interest throughout the system.

Environmental Hazards

Be aware of each planet's unique dangers, from crushing gravity to deadly creatures and extreme temperatures.
